Cavaliers Homepage

"All In"

This motto was for the 2016 NBA season, the cleveland cavaliers were determined to do what ever it took to win the championship. The motto started from the owner Dan Gilbert making a comment on saying he is all in he is putting everything on the line and leaving nothing on the table. Everyone else in the organization soon followed what Dan was preaching and held nothing back doing anything and everything they could to better themselves to win the championship.
